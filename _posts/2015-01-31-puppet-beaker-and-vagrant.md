After test has ended, do:

&nbsp;

$ vagrant global-status

&nbsp;

&nbsp;

this will list all the vagrant machines created by beaker. Then use the id, to destroy these vagrant vms:

&nbsp;

$ vagrant destroy {id}

&nbsp;
